The Best Movies according to Anju

My Top 3 Movies of all Time

Vettam

Vettam ( transl. Light) is a 2004 Indian Malayalam-language screwball comedy film directed by Priyadarshan

Neram

Neram ( transl. Time) is a 2013 Indian black comedy thriller film written, directed and edited by Alphonse Puthren in his directorial debut.

CID Moosa

C.I.D Moosa is a 2003 Indian Malayalam-language action comedy thriller film directed by Johny Antony

OSO

Ohm Shanthi Oshaana is a 2014 Indian Malayalam-language coming-of-age romantic comedy film co-written and directed by Jude Anthany Joseph
